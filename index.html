1 HER

HER is a player-choice-based game that puts the story's outcome into the player's hands. I designed and built this game in University, alongside three other women. The game used movement and point-and-click mechanics, has multiple branching storylines, and nine distinct endings.

2 Bustle

Bustle was a full mockup of a cell phone app that would also users to look at bus schedules, busses in their area, and bus routes, as well as pay for their/family member's bus rides.

UX Prototyping ‧ Adobe XD ‧ Adobe Illustrator ‧ User Testing

3 Let's Get Quizzical

Let's Get Quizzical is a website built to create and share quizzes (in the style of early-2010s Buzzfeed) that also incorporate social media functions (like friends lists, private messaging, and timelines).
